Sound Menu

User's Guide > Menu Reference > Sound Menu

Setting of sound output, etc.

Play Selected Region

Play the selected region. Same as clicking the middle mouse button.

Play Window Contents

Play the current displayed area.

Play Entire File

Play the entire file.

Stop Processing

Stop the current processing.

Enable Loop Play

Enable loop play. The area will be played endlessly until you stop playing. You can change the area by moving the edge of the selection.

Enable Synchronous Play

Enable autoscroll synchronized with playing area. The displayed area will be autoscrolled with the current playing area.

Pause cursor of this window

Pause the cursor of the current window. The cursor starts moving by playing and keeps current position by stopping playing.
